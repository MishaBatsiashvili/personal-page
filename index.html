About

Back in 2018, I started my journey as a Full Stack Developer at Storera. Fast-forward to today, and I've contributed to over 30 projects across startups and enterprises, spanning logistics, e-commerce, and SaaS. Now, I'm currently focused on AI agent development and architecting highly scalable cloud-native platforms.

My engineering philosophy is rooted in moving beyond the chatbot. If an AI strategy is limited to a chat window and a 'Send' button, it's operating in the past. The future lies in workflows and architecting autonomous 'reasoning loops'. No single LLM rules them all, which is why I orchestrate between models based on their strengths: GPT for logic, Claude for extensive context extraction, and Gemini for rapid tool-calling.

At Cykel, I build the full-stack platform behind autonomous digital workers for sales and recruitment. Running these 'thinking' loops on GCP Cloud Functions and AWS ECS Fargate requires rigorous state management—it's not just about caching a profile, but persisting the state of a multi-step autonomous thought process.

When I'm not untangling complex asynchronous state or optimizing serverless architectures, I'm usually advocating for deterministic UI tools that bridge the gap between abstract LLM prompt chains and human-auditable workflows.
